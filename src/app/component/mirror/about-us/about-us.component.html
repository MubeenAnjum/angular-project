About Us

Our Story

At Mirrors Institute of Aesthetics, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of beauty and aesthetic treatments and techniques. From skincare and makeup artistry to advance courses are designed to equip students with the expertise needed to meet the diverse needs of their future clients. Our programs are continuously updated to reflect the latest trends, techniques and innovations in the beauty industry, ensuring that our graduates are always at the forefront of their profession.

Who We Are

Mirrors Institute of Aesthetics, Approved by CIDESCO, CIBTAC, ITEC, DHA and KHDA. A premier destination for certified beauty education in Dubai. Our beauty institute is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of aestheticians, providing them with the knowledge, skills, and hands-on experience necessary to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of beauty industry.

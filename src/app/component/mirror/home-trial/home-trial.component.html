About us

Our beauty school in Dubai is CIDESCO, CIBTEC, ITEC, DHA, KHDA-certified. All of our beautician courses are only available to women at our beauty training centre. Mirrors Institute of Aesthetics will assist you in launching your career in the highly profitable beauty industry. Our Specialists have planned this course focusing on the techniques which are exceptionally required in the present current industry. The instructor will take you through all the steps of application and maintenance, as well as the most proficient method to talk with customers for ideal outcomes. You will learn about the techniques, confidence, and skills. After completing our parlour courses, Our beauty institute provides a beautician course diploma and you will be professionally developed to maintain a positive attitude, polished methodology at your workplace. All these modern techniques, smart facilities at Mirrors Institute of Aesthetics also additionally underlines on how to make you a successful beauty therapist in the market.

Frequently asked questions

What is a keratin hair treatment?

Keratin is a protein in our body that naturally occurs that makes up a large amount of skin, hair, & nails. In spite of the fact that the keratin cells die with the time, it’s essential that they are cared for. The keratin protein makes up about 95% of our hair, so taking care of keratin extremely important. For hair, healthy keratin healthy keratin production is essential for beautiful, shiny hair.

Does Brazilian keratin treatment reduce the natural volume of the hair?

No, your hair will retain most of its natural volume after the Brazilian Keratin Treatment. Using a Curling iron and round brush will always be the best option to create more volume and body.

Will my hair be straight after the keratin treatment done?

If your hair is straight, it will eliminate the frizzy hair and give you a healthier and shiny look. If the hair is curly, it will eliminate the frizzy hair and define the natural curl. It always depends on the texture of your hair.

How do I know if I can undergo keratin hair treatment?

Keratin treatment is an option for everyone and it suits every hair type. It will be equally effective on all hair types including thick, thin, dry, chemically damaged, straight, wavy and curly. For pregnant customers we advise that they should get doctor’s approval since prevention is always better.

How long does the keratin treatment last?

The results will depend on the types of hair and how often they are washed. It normally lasts up to 12 weeks however the wave can return sooner

How do I maintain my hair after a keratin treatment?

The main important thing that can be used to appropriate aftercare product line that your salon or stylist suggests is that you need to wash your hair around 3 times per week if possible and follow the advice of your beautician for other hair treatments that you can do or not or how long will the waiting period is.

Will swimming be compromise my keratin hair treatment?

Swimming is fine but to protect the hair from chlorine and salt in the water, you should discuss with your stylist & use some product to apply before swimming to protect the hair. Otherwise, the life of your treatment may be compromised if you swim regularly without any hair protection.

. Is the keratin hair treatment permanent?

No, it is not permanent. But creates a protein layer overlay on the cuticle which protects the hair for an extended time, so the condition is improved.
